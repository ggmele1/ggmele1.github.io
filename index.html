My Name Is Giuseppe Mele

I like to think of myself as a Multipotentialite. Someone who thrives on learning, exploring, and mastering new skills. Later in life, I was accepted into Columbia College to pursue a future in musical performance. After the usual back and forth mind changing, I decided to turn down the opportunity and began a career in finance. At twenty years of age, I found myself working for the second largest Residential Mortgage lender in America. Although this did not turn out to be my passion, it led me to where I am now. I realized quickly my millennial-self worked best using one of the most complex yet familiar tools, THE INTERNET! Within a year, I switched my position from Loan Officer, to Digital Marketing Specialist. This is where my journey to developing began! January 1st, I quit my job and started learning the necessary tools needed to pursue my new career. A new career that most importantly I was passionate about. One where I could use my creativity and where I can unapologetically... be my best self. Giuseppe The Developer was born!
